This Scheme of Work identifies where GCSE Computer Science topics directly overlap with content students encounter in Maths, Science and Business. The aim is to allow departments to coordinate timing so students meet the same underlying concept — place value, logic, data handling, systems thinking — in multiple subjects within the same half-term, strengthening retention and transfer. Each unit below is expandable and contains topic-level mappings with suggested cross-curricular activities.
